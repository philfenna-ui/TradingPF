Controls weighting style and aggressiveness. Lower values (0.00-0.35) bias toward defensive/macro-stable signals and reduce aggressive setups. Mid values (0.36-0.65) balance growth and protection. Higher values (0.66-1.00) emphasize momentum/options/short-term opportunity and can increase turnover and volatility. Controls how modules are weighted in ranking. Balanced: equalized mix of trend, macro, volatility, and catalyst signals. Conservative: prioritizes macro stability, volatility control, and defensive quality; typically lower turnover. Aggressive: prioritizes momentum, technicals, options, and short-term catalysts; can raise volatility and turnover. If results feel too reactive, move toward Conservative; if too slow, move toward Aggressive.
